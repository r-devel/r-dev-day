# R Dev Day @ Australia 2025

## Participants

This was a standalone event, with the majority attending in-person at Monash University, Melbourne; 
some people joining in-person hubs at the University of Queensland, Brisbane and The Kids Research Institute Australia, Perth, 
and a few others joining remotely.
There were 79 people that registered, with 35 participating on the day.
Of these participants, 22 (63%) identified as belonging to one or more groups traditionally under-represented in the developer community.

# R Dev Day @ New Zealand 2025, 16-17 December 2025

## Participants

This was a standalone hybrid event, with the University of Auckland as the in-person venue.
In order to accommodate a range of time zones, the event ran as two streams: morning or afternoon local time, over two days.
Participants could attend one or both streams; 15 people participated altogether.

Due to the small numbers, EDIA summaries are not reported for this individual event (they will be summarised over multiple R Dev Days).
